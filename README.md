### What is the difference between SEO and AEO for cybersecurity companies?

SEO targets visibility and click-through traffic from Google's organic rankings. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) targets your presence inside AI-generated responses on platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews — where security professionals increasingly research vendors, threat categories, and technology comparisons. In 2026, the most effective cybersecurity SEO consultants build for both simultaneously, ensuring your brand is cited as an authoritative source regardless of where buyers conduct their research.
